Fashionable textiles & Time in text

How we designed a unique Watch-Face for Google’s Wear OS Fall Fashion Watch-Faces collection.

  • Deliverables:UX-UI
  • Platforms:Wear OS

Hands and Digits OUT- Fonts IN

Wearable devices, especially Smartwatchs, are fun and have beautiful, colorful screens... and they are SMART. The creativity and theory behind Textile watch face started from a fundamental understanding that it is no longer necessary to show the time by hands or digits. The smartwatch screen can project vivid, colorful Images, typography, and fonts.

So NOW the watch can show or “tell” the time in words.

“Showing” or “Telling” the hour

We set two ways to view and see the time. “Showing” - the first way, represents a casual approch of saying the time. “Telling” - the seconed way, represents the English appropriete way of telling hour.

Textile backgrounds

From jeans to silk and all other colorful fabrics available, anyone can choose what they love, and now the watch becomes an even more fashionable accessory with the ability to change its background to amazing textiles that match your style.
